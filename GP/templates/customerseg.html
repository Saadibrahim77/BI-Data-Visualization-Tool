Customer Segmentation Analysis

This service provides an accurate clustering and segmenting of the customers base using the orders data of your oganization, through the RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) scores of each customer and clustering algorthims within a certain time interval. It will help your organization understand its customers better and customizing targeted campaigns and sales techniques.

Required data: (CSV)

Orders csv file containing the following columns with the same names:

  • customer_id
  • customer_name
  • order_id
  • amount_paid
  • created_date
